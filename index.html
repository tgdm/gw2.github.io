Gear


The key here is to get as much damage in as you feel comfortable with. I would recommend Marauder's stats (as shown above) for your armor. You could try playing with Berserker's stats if you're feeling spicy, but you will be squishy.

Marauder is also recommended for your weapon, though Berserker can work if you feel comfortable with the loss in health.

For your trinkets and back item, stick with Berserker. If you have the resources, go for Mighty (+5 Power) Infusions.



Runes & Sigils


For Runes: I recommend Strength Runes as an all-around option. Scholar will be iffy for uptime, Eagle can be a good option as well.

For Sigils: There's room for preference. I'm using Force + Bloodlust, you might want to try out your second slot with Air / Fire / Strength for offense or Blood / Energy for defensive.



Skill Bar

Ether Renewal for condi clear, Arcane Brilliance for damage and blasting finisher.

Lightning Flash and Twist of Fate are going to be necessary to help you position and be safe. These two should be on your skill bar at all times.

Glyph of Storms can be subbed out for a more defensive option. Use this while attuned to Fire for FireStorm to pile onto downs. Earth attunement's Sandstorm can be useful for blinding, avoid Air/Water unless you're certain you can make use of it.

All of your Elite skill options are meh. Conjure Fiergy Greatsword offers some more burst damage if you're caught on rotation or need an escape tool.

Specs

Fire: 1/1/1 for best damage, 3/1/1 for more sustain, 1/1/3 for utility.

Air: 3/3/1 for best damage.

Weaver: 3/2/1 for some rounded, 1/2/1 for best damage, 3/3/1 for survivability.



Food/Oil

Keep an eye out for cost-efficient options with same or equivalent stats. Scoop of Mintberry Swirl Ice Cream is a much cheaper option to Omnomberry Ghost, Slice of Candied Dragon Roll. On the other hand, Slice of Allspice Cake offers Power instead of Precision and could be better, though much more tedious to acquire.
If you want to try to go MAX POWER, Carrot Soufflé is an option.... if you can sustain well enough without the heal-on-crit food.



General Play

Primary damage attunement will be F/E so you can throw out Lava Fonts and Pyroclastic Blast for damage. This also leaves Unsteady Ground on your bar for lines.

Switch to F/F when dropping Meteor Shower (meatballs) to utilize bonus damage from Weaver traits. Rotate back into F/E if you need sustained damage, work toward F/A if you need to chase.

When out of combat and chasing the tag, try to stay in A/x for the movement speed buff.

A good opener to be in before a fight starts is W or F /A so you can throw out Windborne Speed and Static Field before a fight. If W/A, Monsoon pierces targets and chills with 1200 range which can be useful, but puts W into secondary which is less useful for damage, but can give utility thanks to the large water field and chill.

This is the standard Scepter/Focus pick build.
