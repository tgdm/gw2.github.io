General Play

First things first: you're going to want to adjust some gameplay settings to ensure you get the most bang for you buck. Under General Options, look for the "Combat/Movement" section and make sure the following settings are set:
  • Autotargeting: DISABLED
  • Allow Skill Retargeting: ENABLED
It's up to you if you want to try to Lock or Snap ground targeting, but I highly recommend the two above settings. Autotargeting will be a problem with movement-based abilities and might turn you to face a direction you do not want to be going in. Skill Retargeting is incredibly useful when it comes to abilities with cast or channel times, such as
. Without having Skill Retargeting enabled, your abilities will start and finish on the target you initially had targeted - or on nothing if you had nothing targeted. It is much easier to cleave down targets while running or shifting to a priority target with this setting enabled.
